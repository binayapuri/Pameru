About Pameru

Pameru exists to close the gap between engineering intent and site reality on Australian projects. We combine disciplined construction delivery with software that respects how work actually gets done — muddy boots, tight programmes, and multi‑layered compliance.

Operating principles

  • Safety first — design and supervise so workers go home unharmed.
  • Truth on site — one diary, one variation register, one set of approved drawings.
  • Local rules — templates and checks reflect the state or territory of execution.
  • Transparent commercial — clear scope boundaries and documented decisions.

Acknowledgement of Country

Pameru acknowledges the Traditional Custodians of the lands and waters on which we live and work across Australia. We pay respects to Elders past and present and extend that respect to all First Nations peoples.

Sustainable building also means caring for Country — we encourage low‑impact construction practices, responsible waste streams, and designs that reduce operational carbon over the life of the asset.

National codes & standards

Australian building work is governed by the National Construction Code (NCC), referenced standards (for example AS/NZS suites), and state/territory building Acts and regulations. Our teams are accustomed to working with performance solutions, deemed‑to‑satisfy pathways, and staged certification common in modern developments.

Design coordination

Architectural, structural, fire, hydraulic, mechanical, and electrical discipline alignment.

Quality systems

ITPs, hold points, witness points, and as‑built records structured for ISO 9001 style audits.

Handover

O&M manuals, training, defects liability tracking, and warranty registers.
