Pameru CRM — built for construction, not generic sales pipelines

A static marketing preview of our product surface. The live CRM connects pre‑construction (leads, tenders, estimates) with delivery (site diaries, defects, variations) and handover (O&M packs, warranties) — with Australian compliance artefacts stored against the right job and jurisdiction.

Core modules

Configure only what you need: principal contractor programmes differ from specialist subcontractor workflows, but everyone benefits from a single source of truth on site.

🎯

Pipeline & tendering

Opportunity stages, bid deadlines, design responsibility matrix, and RFIs linked to discipline owners — export to PDF or CSV for governance boards.

📅

Programme & lookahead

Milestones, constraints, and weather risk notes. Link crane lifts, concrete pours, and inspections to the programme so the site office and client stay aligned.

📷

Site diary & evidence

Time‑stamped photos, delivery dockets, pre‑start minutes, and visitor logs with structured metadata for disputes and insurance events.

🪪

Subcontractor compliance

Track insurances, workers compensation, SWMS approvals, and trade licences with expiry alerts — scoped to the state or territory where each package is executed.

📐

Variations & EOTs

Controlled registers linking scope changes to cost and programme impacts, with approval chains that mirror your contract administration process.

🌐

Client & consultant portal

Read‑only views for owners and designers: progress, quality records, and commissioning checklists without exposing your full commercial stack.

Data residency & trust

Australian construction teams routinely handle personal information, subcontractor details, and commercially sensitive bids. Architect your production deployment with Australian Privacy Principles (APPs) in mind, role‑based access, and retention policies suitable for litigation holds and defect liability periods.

Implementation checklist

  • Master data: cost codes, cost centres, and project WBS.
  • Templates: SWMS library mapped to high‑risk activities.
  • Integrations: accounting export, document control links.
  • Training: site foremen, project admins, and executives.
